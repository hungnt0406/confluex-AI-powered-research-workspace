\section{Methods}
\label{sec:methods}

\subsection{Dataset}
We evaluate on the MIMIC-III clinical QA split released by Pampari et al.~\cite{pampari2018emrqa},
restricted to discharge summaries with at least one structured
diagnosis code. Following \cite{soni2023clinicalqa}, we exclude
notes with fewer than 200 tokens and de-duplicate by patient ID,
yielding 18{,}241 question--answer pairs across 9{,}812 unique notes.

\subsection{Retriever}
The dense retriever is a domain-adapted MedCPT encoder
\cite{jin2023medcpt} fine-tuned for an additional 3 epochs on
in-domain query--passage pairs. Passages are chunked at
512 tokens with a 64-token stride, a setting we
selected after preliminary sweeps over {256, 512, 1024} tokens.

\subsection{Generator and Baselines}
Generation uses Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct with greedy decoding.
We compare against three baselines: (1) closed-book generation,
(2) BM25 retrieval, and (3) a generic Contriever retriever
\cite{izacard2022contriever}.
