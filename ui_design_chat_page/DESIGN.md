# Evergreen Stewardship

## Design Philosophy
This design system is built on a foundation of reliability, organic professionalism, and clear communication, now optimized for high-performance dark mode environments. By pairing a traditional serif with a modern high-performance sans-serif, it bridges the gap between heritage and contemporary utility. The visual language is grounded in deep botanical tones and light-catching neutrals, suggesting a brand that is established, sustainable, and trustworthy even in low-light digital contexts.

## Typography
The typographic scale is designed for maximum clarity and intellectual authority.
- **Headlines**: Noto Serif. Chosen for its classic proportions and excellent legibility, providing a sense of gravitas and editorial history.
- **Body & Labels**: Inter. A functional, neutral workhorse that ensures complex information remains accessible and easy to parse across all digital interfaces.

## Color Palette
The color strategy utilizes a dark-mode fidelity-based approach to maintain the integrity of the brand's primary signature while ensuring accessibility.
- **Primary Color**: #32432C (Deep Forest Green). Used for key brand moments and primary actions to evoke stability and growth.
- **Neutral Base**: #F9F8F6 (Warm Linen). In this dark-themed application, this light neutral serves as a high-contrast element for text and critical surface highlights, ensuring premium legibility against darker backgrounds.

## UI Geometry & Space
The system moves away from exaggerated editorial spacing toward a more efficient, balanced layout.
- **Roundedness**: 1 (Subtle). Corners feature very slight softening. This maintains a structured, professional appearance while avoiding the harshness of absolute sharp edges.
- **Spacing**: 2 (Normal). The layout follows a standard, balanced density. It provides enough room for elements to breathe without sacrificing information density, making it suitable for both content-heavy dashboards and marketing pages.
